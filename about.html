About Tarot

The earliest examples we have of tarot cards are from Europe in the mid-15th century. It wasn't until the 18th century that tarot cards started to be used for divination (prior to that they had been used for card games like tarocchini and königrufen).

As they're used for divination today, tarot cards can answer specific questions, be used for general guidance, or focused on a specific area of life.

Tarot is not a method of divination that's well suited to tell the future. Where tarot shines is in giving us new perspective on a situation or relationship and a way to connect to messages from your guides and the universe.

To phrase a question for tarot you'll want to keep your question open ended, focused on yourself and your feelings, and phrased positively.
